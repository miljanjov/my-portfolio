Hi there, I am Miljan.

I began my career as a sales in the automotive parts retail industry, where I've been working for the last 9 years. This job taught me how to communicate with people of different personalities and how important it is to quickly respond to clients' requests, because the market is dynamic and ruthless. Over the years, we were introduced to bunch of different softwares that were supposed to make our job easier, but those tools were very often hard to learn and use, with minimal documentation available. So, I spent more and more time looking into issues and wondering why it's so hard to develop something that looks so easy to me? I started to be curious how that actually works and reachout to some of my friends who were developers. And that's where my programming journey started...
